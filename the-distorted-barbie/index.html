the Distorted Barbie, 1996, web domain

The work itself is a multiple page website that explores the idea of Barbie as a capitalist center. The website features multiple images of photoshopped barbies representing different ideas. There is also a lot of exploration of symbolism as a whole and a heavy critique on the barbie in general. The website made Mattel pretty mad and they confronted Mark with a cease and desist order, leading him to restructure certain elements of the piece.
